AIM & ACCOMPLISHMENTS

To promote the learning and exchange of ideas related to technology, automation and software system, a technical society “INGENIUM” is established by the students of G. B. Pant Engineering College. The society provides a platform to nurture the dormant skill of the students through different technical events, workshops, seminars and technical festivals.The prime focus is the drive where student come up with new ideas and bring them in implementation during the competitions.

The Society root out all the hassles faced by the students and involve students with their integrated tools and tasks to perform better.INGENIUM assists students for preparing themselves for purposeful participation in the programmes like ROBOCON, SAE-BAJA, SUPRA and in other Events & Workshops organised in and outside of the college. Through society “INGENIUM”, it is believed that the students will attain the major lines envisage a long voyage in explorations and will build the reputations of our college and its technocrats to an elevation.

Management

INGENIUM is entirely managed by students of GBPEC, New Delhi with a three-tier team structure. The core committee consists of students who overlook all the aspects and activities with over 100 volunteers working in sync to execute and implement the splendid spectacle.
