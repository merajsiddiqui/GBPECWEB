AIM & ACCOMPLISHMENTS

INCEPTUM is the annual technical festival of G. B. Pant Engineering College, New Delhi. It is an initiative of GBPEC students with the idea of spreading the horizon of young and talented minds to a new zenith of technological advancements and creativity. It has been a wonderful journey of 3 years, spreading the light of knowledge to the farthest corner of the country with over 6000 budding engineers in attendance and a reach of over 100 colleges.

INCEPTUM has been the stage to forecast the talents and skills of brilliant minds in helping the society to lead to a path of betterment. Apart from enlightening the world with the light of knowledge the fest has also served the purpose of making the young minds explore their new limits by working within a competitive ambience of diversified talented individuals and groups. Also a large number of technical, non technical events& workshops are organized every year under INCEPTUM.

Management

INCEPTUM is entirely managed by students of GBPEC, New Delhi with a three-tier team structure. The core committee consists of students who overlook all the aspects and activities with over 100 volunteers working in sync to execute and implement the splendid spectacle.
